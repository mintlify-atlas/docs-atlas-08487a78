# Creating Your Fitness Plan

JCV Fitness uses a comprehensive 9-step wizard to create your personalized fitness and nutrition plan. This guide walks you through each step of the process.

## Starting the Wizard

To create your plan:

1. Visit the JCV Fitness website at [jcv24fitness.com](https://jcv24fitness.com)
2. Click the **"Crear Tu Rutina"** or **"Start Wizard"** button
3. You'll be taken to `/wizard` to begin the personalization process

<Note>
  You don't need to create an account before starting the wizard. Account creation happens after payment.
</Note>

## The 9-Step Wizard Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1: Fitness Level">
    Select your current fitness level:
    
    - **Beginner** - New to exercise or returning after a long break
    - **Intermediate** - Regular exercise experience (3-6 months)
    - **Advanced** - Consistent training for over 6 months
    
    This helps customize the intensity and complexity of your workout plan.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 2: Goals">
    Choose your primary fitness goal:
    
    - **Weight Loss** (Pérdida de peso)
    - **Muscle Gain** (Ganancia muscular)
    - **Toning** (Tonificación)
    - **General Fitness** (Acondicionamiento general)
    
    Your goal determines the workout structure and nutrition macros.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 3: Available Time">
    Select how much time you can dedicate per workout session:
    
    - 20 minutes
    - 30 minutes
    - 45 minutes
    - 60 minutes
    - 90+ minutes
    
    This ensures your plan fits your schedule.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 4: Equipment">
    Select the equipment you have access to:
    
    - **Casa** (Home) - Bodyweight exercises
    - **Gimnasio** (Gym) - Full equipment access
    - **Mínimo** (Minimal) - Dumbbells and resistance bands
    
    Your plan will only include exercises you can actually perform.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 5: Program Duration">
    Choose how long you want your program to run:
    
    - 4 weeks
    - 8 weeks
    - 12 weeks
    
    Longer programs include progressive overload and periodization.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 6: Body Data">
    Enter your personal metrics for accurate calculations:
    
    - **Weight** (kg)
    - **Height** (cm)
    - **Age** (years)
    - **Gender**
    - **Activity Level** (sedentary to very active)
    
    This data calculates your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) and macros.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 7: Exercise Selection">
    Review and customize the exercises in your plan:
    
    - Browse through recommended exercises
    - Each exercise shows target muscles and difficulty
    - Swap exercises you don't like
    - Mark exercises you want to include
    
    The system shows exercises based on your equipment selection.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 8: Food Selection">
    Customize your meal plan:
    
    - Review recommended foods for your goal
    - Mark foods you like or want to include
    - Flag foods you're allergic to or dislike
    - See portion sizes and macros
    
    Your nutrition plan will prioritize the foods you selected.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 9: Plan Summary">
    Review your complete plan summary:
    
    - See your workout structure
    - View meal plan overview
    - Review calculated macros (protein, carbs, fats)
    - Check estimated calories
    - See your selected exercises and foods
    
    If everything looks good, proceed to select your subscription plan.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Selecting Your Subscription

After completing the wizard, you'll see three subscription options:

### Plan Básico - $49,900 COP/month
- 7-day meal plan
- Home workout routine
- App access
- Email support

### Plan Pro - $89,900 COP/month (Most Popular)
- Personalized meal plan
- Gym + home routines
- Exercise videos
- Priority support
- Weekly progress tracking

### Plan Premium - $149,900 COP/month
- Everything in Pro
- 1-on-1 coaching
- Monthly plan adjustments
- VIP community access
- Results guarantee

<Tip>
  The **Plan Pro** is recommended for most users as it includes personalized plans and video guidance.
</Tip>

## What Happens Next?

After selecting a plan:

1. You'll be prompted to create an account or log in (if you haven't already)
2. Proceed to payment via MercadoPago or Wompi
3. After successful payment, your subscription activates immediately
4. Access your complete plan in the dashboard

## Your Wizard Data

Your wizard responses are:

- Saved automatically as you progress (in local storage)
- Migrated to your account after payment
- Used to generate your personalized PDF plan
- Accessible in your dashboard for reference

<Warning>
  Don't close your browser before completing payment, or your wizard data may be lost. The data is stored locally until you create an account.
</Warning>

## Modifying Your Plan

To create a new plan with different parameters:

1. Go to your Dashboard
2. Click **"Create New Plan"** or return to `/wizard`
3. Complete the wizard again with new selections
4. Your new plan will replace the previous one

<Note>
  You can create a new plan anytime during your active subscription at no additional cost.
</Note>
