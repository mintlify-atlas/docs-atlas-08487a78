# Downloading Your Plans

Once you have an active JCV Fitness subscription, you can access and download your personalized plans. This guide explains how to view and download your workout and meal plans.

## Accessing Your Plans

You can access your plans in three ways:

### From the Dashboard

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Dashboard">
    After logging in, go to `/dashboard`. You'll see your **Quick Actions** section.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Plan Type">
    Click one of the quick action buttons:
    
    - **"Ver Plan de Alimentación"** - View your meal plan
    - **"Ver Rutina de Ejercicios"** - View your workout plan
    - **"Descargar PDF"** - Download complete plan as PDF
  </Step>

  <Step title="Plan Opens">
    You'll be taken to:
    
    - `/plan/alimentacion` - Meal plan view
    - `/plan/ejercicios` - Workout plan view
    - `/plan/download` - PDF download page
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Direct URL Access

If you're already logged in with an active subscription:

- **Meal Plan:** `/plan/alimentacion`
- **Workout Plan:** `/plan/ejercicios`
- **Combined View:** `/plan/view`
- **Download Page:** `/plan/download`

<Note>
  All plan pages are protected. You must have an active subscription to access them.
</Note>

### From Email

After payment, you receive a confirmation email with:

- Direct link to your dashboard
- Quick access buttons to plans
- Download instructions

## Downloading Your Plan as PDF

### Download Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Download Page">
    Navigate to `/plan/download` from your dashboard or click **"Descargar PDF"**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Subscription Status">
    The page shows:
    
    - Current subscription status (Active/Inactive)
    - Days remaining in subscription
    - Plan type (Básico, Pro, Premium)
    
    <Warning>
      You must have an active subscription to generate PDFs. Expired subscriptions cannot download.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review Watermark Notice">
    Your PDF will include a watermark with:
    
    - Your full name (or email if no name set)
    - Download date
    - "Personal and Non-transferable" notice
    
    This protects the content and prevents unauthorized sharing.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Download">
    Click the **"Descargar PDF"** button. The system:
    
    1. Verifies your active subscription
    2. Generates a watermarked PDF on-demand
    3. Logs the download with IP and timestamp
    4. Delivers the PDF to your browser
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save Your PDF">
    The PDF downloads with filename: `plan-jcv-fitness.pdf`
    
    Save it to your device for offline access.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### PDF Contents

Your generated PDF includes:

1. **Cover Page**
   - JCV Fitness branding
   - Your name and plan type
   - Subscription dates
   - Personal watermark

2. **Program Overview**
   - Your fitness goals
   - Body metrics and calculations
   - TDEE and macro targets
   - Program duration

3. **Workout Plan**
   - Complete weekly schedule
   - Exercise descriptions
   - Sets, reps, rest periods
   - Progression guidelines

4. **Meal Plan**
   - 7-day meal rotation
   - Complete recipes
   - Macro breakdown per meal
   - Food exchange tables

5. **Appendix**
   - Exercise glossary
   - Nutrition tips
   - Progress tracking logs
   - Support contact information

## Security & Watermarking

### Why Watermarks?

JCV Fitness watermarks all PDFs to:

- Prevent unauthorized distribution
- Protect intellectual property
- Ensure accountability
- Track plan sharing violations

### Watermark Details

Each PDF page includes:

- **Header Watermark:** Your email/name + date
- **Footer Watermark:** "Licensed to [Your Name] - Valid until [Expiration Date]"
- **Embedded Metadata:** Subscription ID and user ID

<Warning>
  Sharing watermarked PDFs violates terms of service and may result in account termination. Plans are personal and non-transferable.
</Warning>

### Download Limits

To prevent abuse:

- **Maximum 5 downloads per day** per user
- Each download is logged with IP address and user agent
- Suspicious activity (e.g., sharing IPs) may trigger account review

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can't access plan pages">
    **Possible causes:**
    
    - Subscription expired - Renew at `/pricing`
    - Not logged in - Log in at `/login`
    - No subscription yet - Purchase plan first
    
    **Solution:** Check subscription status in `/dashboard`
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="PDF download fails">
    **Try these steps:**
    
    1. Check your internet connection
    2. Verify you haven't hit the 5 downloads/day limit
    3. Try a different browser
    4. Clear browser cache and cookies
    5. Contact support if issue persists
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="PDF is blank or incomplete">
    **Possible issues:**
    
    - PDF generation still in progress (wait 10 seconds)
    - Browser blocked the download
    - PDF reader compatibility issue
    
    **Solution:** Re-download and use Adobe Reader or Chrome PDF viewer
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Watermark shows wrong name">
    **How to fix:**
    
    1. Go to `/settings`
    2. Update your "Full Name" field
    3. Save changes
    4. Download PDF again
    
    The watermark uses your profile name or email as fallback.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Need to re-download after expiration">
    Once your subscription expires, you cannot download PDFs. However:
    
    - Your previous downloads remain valid
    - Renewing your subscription restores download access
    - Your plan data is preserved and will be available when you renew
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
