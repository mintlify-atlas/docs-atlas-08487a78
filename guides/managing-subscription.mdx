# Managing Your Subscription

Your JCV Fitness subscription gives you access to personalized workout and nutrition plans. This guide explains how to manage your subscription effectively.

## Viewing Subscription Status

You can view your subscription details in multiple places:

### Dashboard Overview

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Your Dashboard">
    Navigate to `/dashboard` after logging in. You'll see your subscription card prominently displayed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Subscription Card">
    The subscription card shows:
    
    - **Plan Name** (Básico, Pro, or Premium)
    - **Status** (Active, Expired, or Cancelled)
    - **Expiration Date** - When your subscription ends
    - **Days Remaining** - Countdown to expiration
    - **Renewal Options** - Quick access to renew or upgrade
  </Step>

  <Step title="Settings Page">
    For more detailed information, visit `/settings`:
    
    - Account creation date
    - Current plan type
    - Subscription end date
    - Active subscription status
    - Payment history (coming soon)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding Subscription Status

Your subscription can have one of four statuses:

### Active
- Full access to all plan features
- Can view and download plans
- Progress tracking enabled
- Support access available

**Indicator:** Green badge with "Activo" label

### Expiring Soon (7 days or less)
- Still active, but renewal recommended
- Orange warning banner appears
- All features remain accessible
- Renewal prompts in dashboard

**Indicator:** Orange "Expiring Soon" warning

### Expired
- Subscription period has ended
- Cannot access plans or download PDFs
- Previous data is preserved
- Can reactivate by purchasing new subscription

**Indicator:** Red "Expired" badge

### Cancelled
- User requested cancellation
- Access ends at current period end
- No automatic renewal
- Can reactivate anytime

**Indicator:** Gray "Cancelled" badge

## Renewing Your Subscription

JCV Fitness uses manual renewal (no automatic billing).

<Steps>
  <Step title="Renewal Notification">
    You'll see renewal prompts when:
    
    - 7 days remain (orange banner in dashboard)
    - 3 days remain (email notification)
    - Subscription expires (plan access blocked)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start Renewal Process">
    To renew your subscription:
    
    1. Click **"Renovar ahora"** in the subscription card
    2. Or visit `/pricing` directly
    3. Select your desired plan (can upgrade/downgrade)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Payment">
    - Choose payment method (MercadoPago or Wompi)
    - Complete the payment flow
    - Your subscription activates immediately
    - New expiration date is set (current date + 1 month)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Renewing before expiration ensures uninterrupted access to your plans and progress data.
</Tip>

## Upgrading or Downgrading

You can change your plan tier at any time:

### Upgrading (e.g., Básico → Pro)

1. Visit `/pricing`
2. Select the higher-tier plan
3. Complete payment
4. New plan activates immediately
5. New features become available instantly

<Note>
  When upgrading, you pay the full price of the new plan. The new subscription period starts from the upgrade date.
</Note>

### Downgrading (e.g., Premium → Pro)

1. Let your current subscription expire
2. Purchase the lower-tier plan
3. Or contact support for immediate downgrade options

<Warning>
  Downgrading removes access to premium features (e.g., 1-on-1 coaching, VIP community). Your plan data is preserved but some features may become unavailable.
</Warning>

## Subscription Expiration Handling

When your subscription expires:

### What Happens
- You're redirected to `/plan/expired` when trying to access plans
- Dashboard shows "No active subscription" message
- Your wizard data and preferences are **preserved**
- Previous plans are saved (read-only, no downloads)

### Reactivating

<Steps>
  <Step title="Purchase New Subscription">
    Visit `/pricing` and select any plan tier.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Data Restoration">
    After payment, your previous:
    
    - Wizard preferences are restored
    - Exercise selections are reloaded
    - Food preferences are maintained
    - Body data is carried forward
  </Step>

  <Step title="Plan Access Restored">
    You immediately regain:
    
    - Plan viewing access
    - PDF download capability
    - Progress tracking
    - Support access
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Cancelling Your Subscription

JCV Fitness subscriptions don't auto-renew, so "cancellation" simply means not renewing when your period ends.

### To Cancel

1. Let your subscription expire naturally (no action needed)
2. Don't click renewal prompts
3. Access ends at the expiration date

### If You Need Immediate Cancellation

Contact support:

- **WhatsApp:** 314 382 64 30
- **Email:** Through your dashboard support form
- **Request:** Immediate subscription cancellation

<Note>
  Refunds are handled case-by-case. Contact support within 7 days of purchase for refund requests.
</Note>

## Account Deletion

To permanently delete your account and all data:

1. Go to `/settings`
2. Scroll to **"Zona de peligro"** (Danger Zone)
3. Click **"Contactar soporte"**
4. Request account deletion via WhatsApp or email

<Warning>
  Account deletion is **permanent and irreversible**. All your plans, progress data, and preferences will be permanently deleted.
</Warning>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do subscriptions auto-renew?">
    No. JCV Fitness uses manual renewal. You must purchase a new subscription when your current one expires.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens to my data when my subscription expires?">
    Your wizard data, preferences, and plan history are preserved. You can't access or download plans, but everything is restored when you reactivate.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I pause my subscription?">
    Subscriptions cannot be paused. If you need a break, let your subscription expire and reactivate when ready. Your data will be waiting.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I get a refund?">
    Refunds are evaluated case-by-case within 7 days of purchase. Contact support with your reason and payment details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I share my subscription with others?">
    No. Subscriptions are personal and tied to your email. Plans include watermarks with your name and are non-transferable.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I view my payment history?">
    Payment history is visible in your dashboard under the subscription card. For detailed receipts, contact support.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
