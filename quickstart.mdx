## Get Started in 10 Minutes

This guide walks you through creating your first personalized fitness plan with JCV Fitness. You'll create an account, complete the wizard, and access your customized workout and meal plans.

<Note>
  You can try JCV Fitness with our 5-week trial plans before subscribing. Complete the wizard to see what your personalized program looks like!
</Note>

## Step 1: Create Your Account

Navigate to the JCV Fitness platform and click **Get Started** or **Sign Up**.

### Registration Options

You have two authentication methods:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email & Password">
    1. Enter your email address
    2. Create a secure password
    3. Add your full name (optional)
    4. Click **Sign Up**
    5. Check your email for verification
    
    ```typescript
    // Authentication uses Supabase
    const { data, error } = await supabase.auth.signUp({
      email,
      password,
      options: {
        data: {
          full_name: fullName,
        },
      },
    });
    ```
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Magic Link">
    1. Enter your email address
    2. Click **Send Magic Link**
    3. Check your email inbox
    4. Click the verification link
    5. You'll be automatically logged in
    
    ```typescript
    // Magic link authentication
    const { error } = await supabase.auth.signInWithOtp({
      email,
      options: {
        emailRedirectTo: `${window.location.origin}/auth/callback`,
      },
    });
    ```
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Warning>
  Make sure to verify your email address before proceeding. Check your spam folder if you don't see the verification email within a few minutes.
</Warning>

## Step 2: Complete the 9-Step Wizard

After logging in, you'll be guided through our interactive wizard at `/wizard`. Each step helps us understand your unique fitness profile.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Training Level">
    Select your experience level:
    - **Principiante** - New to fitness (< 3 months)
    - **Básico** - Fundamental knowledge (3-6 months)
    - **Intermedio** - Consistent training (6-18 months)
    - **Avanzado** - High performance (2+ years)
    - **Elite** - Competitive level (4+ years)
    
    This determines exercise difficulty and progression speed.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Training Goal">
    Choose your primary objective:
    - 🔥 Quemar Grasa - Reduce body fat percentage
    - 💪 Ganar Musculo - Increase muscle mass and strength
    - ✨ Tonificar - Define muscles while maintaining athletic physique
    - 🏃 Resistencia - Improve cardiovascular capacity
    - 🧘 Flexibilidad - Increase range of motion
    - 🏋️ Fuerza Pura - Maximize strength in compound lifts
    - ⚡ Más Energía - Feel more vitality daily
    - ❤️ Salud General - Improve overall cardiovascular health
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Time Availability">
    How many days per week can you train?
    - 2-3 days (minimal commitment)
    - 4-5 days (standard program)
    - 6-7 days (intensive training)
    
    Your program will be optimized for this schedule.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Equipment Access">
    Select what equipment you have available:
    - **Casa** - Bodyweight, resistance bands, dumbbells
    - **Gimnasio** - Full gym equipment
    - **Ambos** - Flexibility to train anywhere
    
    This determines which exercises are included in your routine.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Workout Duration">
    How long can each workout session be?
    - 20-30 minutes (quick sessions)
    - 45-60 minutes (standard workouts)
    - 60-90 minutes (extended training)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Body Measurements">
    Enter your current stats:
    - Age (years)
    - Weight (kg)
    - Height (cm)
    - Target weight (optional)
    - Body fat percentage (optional)
    
    This helps calculate your nutritional needs and track progress.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Exercise Preferences">
    Select exercises you want to avoid or focus on:
    - Browse 100+ exercises in our library
    - Mark exercises you can't perform (injuries/limitations)
    - Star exercises you want to prioritize
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Food Preferences">
    Customize your meal plan:
    - Dietary restrictions (vegetarian, vegan, etc.)
    - Allergies and intolerances
    - Foods you dislike
    - Meal frequency preference
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Summary">
    Review all your selections before generating your plan:
    - Verify all information is correct
    - Make any necessary changes
    - Click **Generate My Plan** to create your personalized program
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Your selections are saved in real-time using Zustand state management, so you can leave and return without losing progress.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Your Plan">
    Click **Seleccionar plan** on your preferred subscription tier
  </Step>

## Step 5: View Your Personalized Plans

### Workout Plan

Access your workout routine at `/plan/ejercicios`:

- **Weekly Schedule** - See which days you train and rest
- **Exercise Details** - Sets, reps, rest periods, and tempo
- **Progression System** - Gradual increases in volume and intensity
- **Form Videos** - Watch proper technique for each exercise
- **Alternative Exercises** - Swap exercises based on equipment/preferences

### Meal Plan

View your nutrition program at `/plan/alimentacion`:

- **Daily Meal Schedule** - Breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks
- **Macronutrient Breakdown** - Protein, carbs, fats, and calories
- **Food Exchange Tables** - Swap foods while maintaining nutrition balance
- **Portion Guidance** - Exact measurements for each meal
- **Shopping Lists** - What to buy each week

<Note>
  Both plans adapt to your selections from the wizard. Your training level, goals, equipment, and food preferences all influence the final program.
</Note>

## Step 6: Download Your Plans

You can download PDF versions of your plans for offline access:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Download Page">
    Go to `/plan/download` from your dashboard or plan pages
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Content">
    Choose what to include:
    - Workout plan
    - Meal plan  
    - Exercise library
    - Food exchange tables
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Generate PDF">
    Click **Download Plan** to generate your personalized PDF with:
    - Your name as watermark (prevents unauthorized sharing)
    - All selected content
    - Professional formatting
    - Printer-friendly layout
  </Step>
</Steps>

```typescript
// PDF generation with user watermark
POST /api/plan/generate-pdf

{
  "userId": "user-uuid",
  "planType": "workout",
  "watermark": true
}
```

<Warning>
  PDFs are personalized with your account watermark. Sharing your login or PDFs violates our terms of service and may result in account suspension.
</Warning>

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I change my plan after subscribing?">
    Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your subscription tier at any time from your dashboard. Changes take effect at your next billing cycle.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What if I need to skip a workout or meal?">
    Your plan is flexible. You can adjust timing and swap exercises/meals using the substitution features. Premium subscribers can request custom adjustments from their coach.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How often should I regenerate my plan?">
    We recommend regenerating your plan every 4-6 weeks to account for progress and prevent plateaus. Premium subscribers get monthly adjustments included.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I use the app without a subscription?">
    Yes! The 5-week trial plans are available without a subscription. This lets you test the platform and see sample workouts before committing.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
